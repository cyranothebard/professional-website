BridgeOps Intelligence Layer

Predictive Maintenance Decision Intelligence

Explainable maintenance recommendations for industrial operations.

Executive Summary

This project turns operational data into explainable maintenance actions. It combines health-state modeling, failure-risk forecasting, remaining useful life estimation, and a recommendation engine that tells teams what to do next.

BridgeOps Position

The work belongs in the Intelligence Layer: it sits above the data platform and below future automation. The point is decision support, not autonomous maintenance.

Key Outputs

  • Health states: Healthy, Degraded, Critical, Failure
  • Failure probability and failure-risk score
  • Predicted remaining useful life
  • Recommended action: Monitor, Inspect, Schedule Maintenance, or Immediate Review
  • Explainability via transition probabilities and state evolution

Business Value

The project helps maintenance teams reduce unplanned downtime, prioritize the right assets, and connect technical signals to business value. A small automation preview shows how the recommendation could trigger workflow tooling in the future.
